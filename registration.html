REGISTRATION


Target Audience

The workshop will be a two-day meeting where all the astronomy community from Brazil and Argentina, as well as researchers from Institutions that have collaborated to the LLAMA projects so far, as NOVA (Netherlands), U. Groningen (Netherlands), and Universidad de Chile, are welcomed to participate, by presenting projects and and joining the discussions.
