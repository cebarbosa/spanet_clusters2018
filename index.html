ABOUT THE MEETING


The meeting aims to bring together the various groups that invested or helped the LLAMA project up to now to discuss projects for early science and plans for future surveys that could be performed with the LLAMA antenna. The discussions will focus on finding niches of observations that only LLAMA can perform, possible joint programs, and suggestions for the organization of a time allocation committee.
